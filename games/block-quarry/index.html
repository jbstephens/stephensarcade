BLOCK QUARRY

DIG · BUILD · ESCAPE
Dig down through the quarry. Mine ore, build paths, dodge cave-ins and aliens.
Reach the bottom to grab the Starcrystal and rescue Sammy — then race the collapse back to the surface.
iPad: d-pad to move · tap UP = jump (hold UP to dig the block above) · PLACE drops the highlighted block · TOOL cycles which block · USE opens bench / drops TNT
Keyboard P1: WASD · SPACE place · Q/E cycle · F use · P2 (co-op): arrows · ENTER · ,/. · / · Gamepads supported
CLIMBING BACK UP:
Ladder tower (easiest): tap TOOL until LADDER is lit, tap PLACE (ladder drops in your tile), hold UP to climb, tap PLACE again, hold UP, repeat.
Pillar: hold DOWN + tap PLACE with DIRT or STONE selected — lifts you 1 tile per tap.
The gold-outlined tile always shows where your next PLACE will land.
